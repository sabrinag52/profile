Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You need to visit HZ Print and hand in your file.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

HZ Scan

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

You need to visit Reserve a room and then follow the steps.

How can you park your car at the HZ parking lot?

In order to get into the HZ parking lot and park there you need to reveal your HZ pass at the barriers.
